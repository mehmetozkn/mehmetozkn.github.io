Belagat Privacy Policy

At Belagat ("the App"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data such as your name, physical address, or phone number. We may collect non-personal technical data (e.g., device model, iOS version) to improve the App's performance and stability.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our App and manage subscriptions:

  • Firebase (Google): We may use Firebase to collect anonymous analytics data to detect crashes and improve user experience.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscription infrastructure and purchase history. All payment transactions are processed by Apple; we do not have access to or store your credit card information.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
